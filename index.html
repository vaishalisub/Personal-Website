September 2022 - February 2023

ACE Mentorship Program

During my junior year of highschool, I applied for the Architecture, Construction, and Engineering (ACE) Mentorship Program.This is an opportunity for highschool students to get connected with mentors and like minded peers in the ACE fields. This program aims to inspire highschool students to pursue careers in Architecture, Construciton, and Engineering fields by having them collaborate on a project throughout the year. For my year, our project was to collaborate with my team and mentors to create a physical and digital model of an Arcade that met the requirements of a sample client. After we finished our models, we presented them to judges and were awarded second place for our final physical model.
